# Prevention of Sexual Harassment (POSH) 

## 1. What kinds of behavior cause sexual harassment?

* **Unwanted Physical Contact:** Touching, patting, hugging, or any unwelcome physical contact.
* **Demanding Sexual Favors:** Asking for sexual favors in return for job benefits like promotions or good ratings.
* **Verbal Harassment:** Making sexual jokes, comments, or spreading rumors.
* **Non-Verbal Harassment:** Staring inappropriately, making sexual gestures, or whistling.
* **Visual Harassment:** Showing pornography, sexual posters, or sending inappropriate messages/emails.
* **Threats:** Threatening someone's job or career if they refuse sexual advances.

## 2. What would you do in case you face or witness such behavior?

* **Say No Clearly:** Tell the person directly that their behavior is unwelcome and they must stop.
* **Keep Records:** Note down the dates, times, places, and details of the incidents. Save any related emails, messages, or notes.
* **Report the Incident:** File a formal written complaint with the Internal Complaints Committee (ICC) or your HR department immediately.
* **Support the Victim:** If you see it happen, offer support to the victim and encourage them to report it to the ICC.
* **Maintain Confidentiality:** Do not discuss the incident with other colleagues to protect everyone's privacy.
* **Cooperate with Investigation:** Provide true and complete information if the ICC asks you to give a statement.
