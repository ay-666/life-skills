# Grit and Growth Mindset

## 1. Grit
* Grit means having passion and never giving up on long-term goals. 
* It predicts success better than just being smart. 

## 2. Introduction to Growth Mindset

* **Fixed Mindset:** Thinking we are just born with your skills.
* **Growth Mindset:** Believing you can build new skills over time by working hard.

## 3. Understanding Internal Locus of Control

* **What it is:** Believing that we control out life through your own hard work, not luck or outside factors.
* **Key Point:** Believing our actions matter is the best way to stay motivated.

## 4. How to build a Growth Mindset
* Believe we can figure things out.
* Stop assuming we cannot do hard things.
* Plan our own learning path.
* Accept and learn from hard times.

## 5. Mindset - Action Ideas

* **Use Code Reviews:** Treat feedback as a way to learn, not as an insult.
* **Face the Unknown:** Trust that we can search the web and read guides to fix hard bugs.
* **Use Mistakes:** View bugs and errors as clues for what we need to learn next.
* **Keep Learning:** Build side projects to teach ourselves new coding skills.
