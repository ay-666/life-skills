# Tiny Habits & Atomic Habits

## 1. Tiny Habits - BJ Fogg
**Question 1: What was the most interesting idea for you?**
* Attaching a tiny action to something we already do. 
* For example, a small 5 min walk after every meeting. 
* Tiny actions are so easy that they do not need much motivation.

## 2. Tiny Habits Core Message
**Question 2: What is B = MAP?**
* It means Behavior happens when Motivation, Ability, and a Prompt happen at the same time.
* **Motivation:** How much we want to do it.
* **Ability:** How easy it is to do.
* **Prompt:** The reminder to do it (like finishing dinner).

**Question 3: Why is it important to celebrate?**
* Celebrating makes us feel good right away.
* This good feeling rewards our brain. 
* It trains our brain to turn that action into an automatic habit.

## 3. 1% Better Every Day
**Question 4: What was the most interesting idea for you?**
* Small changes add up quickly. 
* If I get just 1% better every day, I will be about 37 times better in one year.

## 4. Book Summary of Atomic Habits
**Question 5: What does the book say about Identity?**
* Change the habits by changing how we see ourselves.
* Instead of saying "I want to build an app," say "I am a coder."

**Question 6: How do you make a habit easier?**
* **Fix your space:** Set up the room so good habits are the easiest choice.
* **The 2-Minute Rule:** Make sure any new habit takes less than two minutes to do.

**Question 7: How do you make a bad habit harder?**
* **Add steps:** Put roadblocks between us and the bad habit.
* **Hide triggers:** Remove things from the room that tempt us.

## 5. Reflection
**Question 8: Pick a habit to do more of. What steps can you take?**
* **Habit:** Running every morning.
* **Cue:** Keep the running outfit and shoes ready the night before.
* **Easy:** Write just do it for 10 min at first.
* **Satisfying:** Mark it done on a calendar to feel proud.

**Question 9: Pick a habit to do less of. What steps can you take?**
* **Habit:** Watching too many YouTube videos.
* **Hard:** Log out of the account so we need to type the password every time.
* **Unsatisfying:** Set a strict 15-minute timer. Stop watching the second it rings.
