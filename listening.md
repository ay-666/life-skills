
# Listening and Active Communication

## Active Listening
1. Strategies:
   * Clearing my mind and carefully listening.
   * Not interrupting the other person.
   * Ask related questions.
   * Show engaged body language.
   * Take notes during important conversations.

## Reflective Listening
2. Key Points:
   * Having the right mindset like not judging others, trying to see things from others' perspective.
   * Talk less.
   * Focus on feelings rather than facts.
   * Match the vibe of the conversation.

## Reflection
3. When I listen to someone, if they are not speaking about the topic I am interested in, then my attention mostly tends to doze off and I cannot take much information from that conversation.
4. I am thinking of starting to listen to some podcasts on a regular basis for 10-15 mins even if the topic doesn't interest me.

## Types of Communication
5. Mostly when I think speaking will cause unnecessary trouble, like when I go with friends and they order something I don't like, I generally don't say anything even if it bothers me.
6. Mostly for me when I feel stressed I tend to be more rude.
7. Mostly when I am angry or dissatisfied with someone or about something.
8. Useful steps:
   * I think I should not judge people based on their opinions.
   * I should also try to maintain more eye-contact with the other person and also focus on my body language.
